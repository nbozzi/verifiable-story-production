## What VSP Solves

Generative AI has entered development, production, post-production, marketing, and distribution.
What is missing is not better output - but **defensible, auditable process integrity**.

VSP enables productions to be defensible:

- in court  
- to clients, broadcasters, and commissioning editors  
- to insurers (e.g. Errors & Omissions)  
- to regulators (e.g. EU AI Act Art. 50)  
- when something goes wrong

## What VSP Is â€” and Is Not

**VSP is:**
- a governance framework  
- a production standard  
- a decision and audit layer  
- tool-agnostic and jurisdiction-aware  

**VSP is not:**
- a software tool  
- a generative model  
- automated legal advice  
- an aesthetic or moral scoring system  

VSP evaluates **defensibility**, not creativity.
